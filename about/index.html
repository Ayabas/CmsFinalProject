ABOUT ME

My name is Dennis Adelmann, and I am a Multi-Disciplinary Designer based in Mannheim/Germany. Since 2011 I am studying Communication Design at the University of Applied Science, Mannheim. I am pleased to offer my talents as a designer to both solo and collaborative projects. My work mainly focuses on Interactive, Web- & UI-Design, Editorial and Corporate Design, as well as Photography and Video.

I am open to every kind of assignment and project, so if you want to work or collaborate with me — don´t hesitate to send me an email.
