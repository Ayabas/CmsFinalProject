ABOUT ME

Coming from two contrasting cultures, Swiss & Saudi Arabian, I have experienced changes throughout my growth in languages, cultures & tastes. One constant in my life is my ability to express my feelings through art & design. I received my BFA in Communication Design & Technology from Parsons, New York & in 2010 I presented my First Solo Art Show at the Westwood Gallery, New York City. I have recently moved to Chicago where I am currently pursuing my MFA in the same field & a certificate of Web Design at the School of Art Institute of Chicago.

To me design is finding harmony in the assemblage of elements. It is no longer stopping when there is nothing more to add, rather more stopping where there is nothing left to take away.
